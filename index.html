What do I do?

I translate text, documents, websites, and apps between languages. I specialize in medical translation. I have three years of bachelor's studies in biomedical sciences, one year of work experience as a pharmacy technician and currently study nursing at the University of Alicante.

I was born in Brazil, grew up in Canada, and currently live in Spain. I am fluent in Portuguese, French, and English. I have a certified B2 in Spanish writing and communication, and a certified C1 in written and oral understanding.

My language pairs are the following

  • French to and from English
  • French to and from Portuguese
  • English to and from Portuguese
  • Spanish to French, English, or Portuguese

Furthermore, I am highly experient in the usage of LaTeX, Typst, and Poppler for typesetting and analysing documents, and offer translations in near-identical formats.

My rates are 0.12-0.16 EUR per word for translation with an extra 0.04 EUR per word for customized formatting. Non-formated translations will still respect the core aspects of the original layout, such as number of columns, text alignment, images, font-type, and font size (serif or non-serif).
